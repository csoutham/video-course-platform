# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Contributors are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss disagreements professionally.
- Focus feedback on code and behavior, not people.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or abusive language.
- Discriminatory comments or conduct.
- Public or private intimidation.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this policy.

## Reporting

To report conduct issues, contact: `chris@cjsoutham.com`.

All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially where possible.
